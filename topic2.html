Web Metrics

Web metrics are quantitative measures used to evaluate the performance and effectiveness of a website.

Essential Metrics

  • Pageviews
  • Bounce rate
  • Average session duration
  • Conversion rate

Example Metrics Comparison

Metric Definition
Pageviews The total number of pages viewed by users.
Bounce Rate The percentage of visitors who leave the site after viewing one page.
